# Power-BI-Project
## Customer Demographics and Shopping Preferences
This Power BI dashboard analyzes customer shopping behavior, purchase patterns, and customer demographics. The project provides insights into purchasing trends, seasonal performance, customer segments, and product category analysis.The dashboard helps businesses understand customer behavior and make data-driven decisions to improve sales and customer satisfaction.

### Objectives
-Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
-Identify high-performing product categories.
-Evaluate seasonal purchasing trends.
-Understand customer demographics.
-Analyze payment methods and locations.
-Measure customer satisfaction through ratings.

### Dataset Features
Customer ID
Gender
Category
Season
Purchase Amount
Review Rating
Payment Method
Previous Purchases
Location
Discount Applied

### Tools & Technologies
Power BI Desktop
Power Query
DAX
Data Modeling
Data Visualization

### Conclusion
The Customer Shopping Insights Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of customer purchasing behavior, product performance, payment preferences, and demographic trends. By combining multiple visualizations and interactive filters, the dashboard enables users to explore customer data effectively and identify meaningful business insights.

The analysis reveals that clothing products contribute the highest sales, male customers account for a larger share of purchases, and seasonal trends significantly influence customer spending. Additionally, payment method preferences and location-based analysis help understand customer behavior more deeply.

Overall, this project demonstrates the use of Power BI, data modeling, DAX, and interactive visualizations to transform raw customer data into actionable insights that can support data-driven business decisions.
